How It Works

Name That Photo changes everything.

No AI guesswork. Just your voice. Label every photo while you're still on site — so your desk work is done before you drive away.

1

Snap the Photo

Take your inspection photo as you normally would — roof, panel, foundation, whatever you're documenting. Nothing changes about how you shoot.

2

Say the Label

Beyond the Basics

Everything else you need on inspection day

Voice naming is the start. Here's what handles the rest of the job, from organizing photos to delivering the finished report.

🗂️ Smart Organization

Photos sort into categories — exterior elevations, roof, interior rooms — and into per-property projects. Drag to reorder, search, or bulk-recategorize anytime.

📄 Branded PDF Reports

Build a claim-ready report on your phone: cover page with your logo and company info, photos grouped by category, page numbers, and an overall narrative field. Attach claim document PDFs — estimates, contracts, or professional reports — directly to the report.

✏️ Crop, Markup & Compass

Crop or annotate any photo on-site. Turn on the compass and every photo is stamped with the direction you were facing — no more guessing "which way was that?"

☁️ Cloud Sync & Backup

Sync a project to Google Drive, pull it down on another device, or send a shareable link to your office or your customer. Save originals straight to your phone's photo library too.

📡 Live View for Homeowners

Stream your inspection in real time so a homeowner or adjuster can watch from anywhere, with two-way audio. Record the session and save it straight to your gallery.

🔍 Document Scanning

Scan or attach a claim form or policy document and the app pulls the claim number and policyholder name automatically — no retyping.

🖥️ Desktop Tools

Drag and drop photos from your desktop into the app to label, crop, and markup them right from your computer — no phone required.
